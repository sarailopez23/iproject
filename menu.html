Our Drinks!

Here are some of the drinks we offer!

Classic Matcha Latte

A traditional blend of ceremonial-grade matcha and steamed milk, lightly sweetened with honey for a smooth and creamy experience.

Horchata Matcha Latte

A delightful twist on the traditional mexican horchata, mixing earthy green tea and cinnamon-spiced, rice-milk sweetness.

Strawberry Matcha Latte

A creamy, refreshing blend of earthy green tea, sweet strawberry, and cold milk layered over ice for a perfectly balanced sip.

Taro Matcha Latte

A chilled, velvety mix of grassy green tea and softly sweet taro, rounded out with milk for a mellow, dessert-like finish.

Mango Matcha Latte

A bright, icy combination of tropical mango sweetness and fresh green tea, softened with milk for a smooth, refreshing finish.

Rose Matcha Latte

A fragrant, lightly floral blend of fresh green tea and delicate rose notes, smoothed with milk for a soft, elegant sip.
